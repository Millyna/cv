Liverpool John Moore’s University

BSc(Hons) Software Engineering - Class 1

My greatest achievement to date would be focused on my final year project. Within this project, I essentially created a small 5-node Raspberry Pi 2 cluster, using this as a test-base for software compatible for the ARMv7 architecture.

  • I used Docker and Kubernetes to implement a user-interface called “Dashboard” on the ARM architecture.
  • I was the “worlds-first” to publish this feature for ARM on the University of Glasgow’s Raspberry Pi Cloud projects blog.
  • Gaining recognition from Google, who then used the blog post to fix issues such as to “allow root” on certain commands and implemented this within their source-code. With this, they also now recognize the ARM community and build an ARM Docker image of their software.
  • With this dissertation I achieved an overall publishable grade of 83%.
